# Flow Trading Skill

## What Is "Unusual Flow"?
Options activity that significantly deviates from normal patterns, suggesting institutional or informed trading. Key indicators:

- **High premium**: Single trade > $100K
- **Volume spike**: Volume > 3x open interest
- **Sweep orders**: Single order split across multiple exchanges (urgency)
- **OTM activity**: Heavy out-of-the-money buying (directional conviction)
- **Concentration**: Same strike/expiry hit repeatedly

## How to Trade Flow

### Bullish Setups
- CALL sweeps with vol/OI > 3
- ATM calls with premium > $250K
- Multiple alerts on same ticker within 1 hour
- ACTION: Buy underlying or follow with calls

### Bearish Setups
- PUT sweeps with vol/OI > 3
- OTM puts (>5% OTM) with high premium
- ACTION: Sell underlying or buy puts

### Avoid
- Quad-witching weeks (noisy hedging flow)
- 24 hours before earnings (IV crush risk)
- Single-leg flows on illiquid tickers
- Flow that contradicts MAG-7 direction

## Position Sizing
- Score 0.4-0.6: 1% of portfolio
- Score 0.6-0.8: 2% of portfolio
- Score 0.8+: 3% of portfolio
- Hard cap: 3% per single flow signal

## Exit Triggers
- Original premium dries up (< 50% of entry day's flow)
- Counter-flow appears (puts after our calls)
- Price hits 1.5x your buy point or stops out
- Time stop: 3 days max for OTM plays
